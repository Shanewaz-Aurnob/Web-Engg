Certificate Withdrawal Endpoints for Students

Endpoints to manage searching and posting data for other students, including retrieval, and submission.

Below are the available endpoints:

  • GET /certificate-withdrawal/?user_id=${user_id}&program_abbr=${program_abbr}: Retrieve all the information of a student related to a certificate withdrawal form.
  • GET /certificate-withdrawal/search-formData/:user_id?pageLimit=${pageLimit}¤tPage=${currentPage}: Retrieve paginated list.
  • POST /certificate-withdrawal/post-form: Submit a new form for certificate.
