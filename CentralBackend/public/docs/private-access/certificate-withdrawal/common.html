Common Endpoint of Certificate Withdrawal

Endpoint to see details of a certificate withdrawal form. It is common for provost, verifier1, verifier2, section-incharge, exam controller, VC and student.

The available endpoint is:

  • GET /certificate-withdrawal-common/see-details/?user_id=${user_id}&form_id=${form_id}: Retrieve a desired single form data according to the role.
