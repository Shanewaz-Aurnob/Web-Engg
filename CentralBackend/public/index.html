Welcome to CSECU Web Server Documentation

Welcome to the REST API (Web server) documentation for the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Chittagong (CSECU). Our API provides seamless and reliable access to the numerous computerized systems and subsystems of the department, allowing them to communicate effectively with our centralized database. In addition, the CSECU web server hosts a variety of static resources, including images and files, enhancing the integration and functionality of departmental applications. Whether you're building new features, integrating systems, or managing data, our API offers robust and efficient solutions tailored to the needs of the CSECU community. Dive into our documentation to discover how you can leverage the CSECU API to streamline your projects and enhance your interactions with our digital infrastructure.
